About Us

Hi! We are a team of four members from Chandigarh University, Mohali. This portfolio is for hosting our in-depth analysis of the statistical data of a bike rental company with lends bikes to the users.

This keeps all the records including the bike ID, source station, docking station, distance, cost and much more. We have analysed the data for five FY from 2015 to 2019 and all the analysis is available on our website.
